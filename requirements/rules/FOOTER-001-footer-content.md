# FOOTER-001 — Footer Content Line

## Summary
The site footer must display a single-line, divider-separated set of links and branding text in the specified order.

## Requirements
1. **Order & Items**
   - The footer must present the following items in this exact order:
     1) Privacy
     2) Terms & Conditions
     3) Contact
     4) FAQs
     5) © 2026 @CalcHowMuch

2. **Single-Line Layout**
   - All items must appear on one straight horizontal line within the footer.
   - Items must not wrap to multiple lines at standard desktop widths.

3. **Dividers**
   - Each adjacent item must be separated by a visible divider using a vertical bar character: `|`.
   - Spacing around dividers must be consistent and readable.

4. **Linking**
   - The first four items (Privacy, Terms & Conditions, Contact, FAQs) must be presented as links.
   - The branding item “© 2026 @CalcHowMuch” is text only.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Footer renders: `Privacy | Terms & Conditions | Contact | FAQs | © 2026 @CalcHowMuch` in a single horizontal line.
- Dividers are visible between each item.
- The four link items remain clickable and clearly distinguishable as links.

## Notes
- Styling should preserve existing footer typography and spacing conventions unless otherwise specified.
