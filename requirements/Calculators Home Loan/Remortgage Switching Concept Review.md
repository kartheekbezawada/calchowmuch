# Remortgage / Switching - Concept Review Pack

This package contains three design directions for the Remortgage / Switching rebuild.

## Scope locked in all concepts
- Single-pane flow.
- Centered page heading.
- No Explanation heading.
- No Additional Fees controls.
- Comparison Horizon as a 2-10 year slider with yearly dot/tick breaks.
- Table block includes Home Loan-style behaviors (sticky header, scrollable table container, monthly/yearly view affordance).
- FAQ block uses the How Much Can I Borrow visual card pattern with 10 numbered cards.

## Direction summary
- Concept A - Aurora Rail
  - Premium dark-glass tone with neon rails and high-energy value accents.
  - Strong wow factor while retaining clear row grouping.
- Concept B - Ledger Flow
  - Editorial and calm with high readability and clean hierarchy.
  - Most minimal and least visually aggressive.
- Concept C - Signal Arc
  - Soft ambient gradients and curved surfaces, between A and B in intensity.
  - Smooth and modern while still visually expressive.
