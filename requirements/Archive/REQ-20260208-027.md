## 6) Compute Logic

### Constants & Defaults
- `MAX_MONTHS = 720` (60 years safety cap)

### Formulas
Uses `calculateBalanceTransfer()` from `/assets/js/core/credit-card-utils.js`:

```
fee = balance * max(0, transferFeePercent) / 100
startingBalance = balance + fee

For each month until balance = 0 or month > MAX_MONTHS:
  activeApr = (month <= promoMonths) ? promoApr : postApr
  monthlyRate = activeApr / 100 / 12
  interest = remaining * monthlyRate
  payment = min(monthlyPayment, remaining + interest)
  principal = max(0, payment - interest)
  remaining = max(0, remaining - principal)
```

Key behavior: During the promo period, interest is calculated at the promo APR (often 0%). After the promo period ends, the post-promo APR kicks in. The transfer fee is added to the starting balance upfront.

### Edge Cases
- Transfer fee = 0%: No fee added; starting balance = transfer balance.
- Promo APR = 0 and promo months covers entire payoff: No interest at all.
- Promo months = 0: Post-promo APR applies from month 1.
- Payment too low to cover post-promo interest: returns error.
- Balance <= 0: error returned.

### Rounding/Precision
- Internal: full precision.
- Display: currency values to 2 decimal places. Months as whole number. Fee percent to 1 decimal.
