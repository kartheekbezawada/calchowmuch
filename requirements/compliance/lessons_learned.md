# lessons_learned.md — Curated Patterns From Past Failures

Purpose
- Capture recurring failure patterns and their fixes.
- Keep this file short and actionable.

Format (append new items at bottom)
- Date: YYYY-MM-DD
- Pattern: what went wrong (1–2 sentences)
- Detection: how to notice it early
- Fix: the minimal change that resolves it
- Prevention: rule/test/checklist item to stop recurrence
