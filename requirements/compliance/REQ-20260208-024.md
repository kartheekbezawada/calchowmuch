## 3) Calculation Rules (Overtime)

Definitions:
- Duration minutes = end − start (+24h if ends-next-day)
- Paid minutes = max(0, duration − unpaid break minutes)

Policies:

### Policy A — Daily overtime
- Daily limit $L_d$ (default 8 hours)
- Daily overtime = max(0, paid_hours − $L_d$)

### Policy B — Weekly overtime
- Weekly limit $L_w$ (default 40 hours)
- Weekly overtime = max(0, total_week_hours − $L_w$)

### Policy C — Daily + weekly combined
- Weekly overtime as in Policy B
- Daily overtime = sum of each day’s overtime (Policy A)
- Total overtime = max(daily_overtime_sum, weekly_overtime)
- Regular hours = total_hours − total_overtime

Night hours (optional, if enabled by UI):
- Night window = 22:00–06:00 (wraps midnight)
- Night hours = overlap of paid time with night window
- Night overtime allocation must be deterministic (e.g., proportional by night share of total hours), and must be documented in the explanation.

Display:
- Do not round during math; display to 2 decimals.
