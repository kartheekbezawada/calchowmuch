How much time is between two dates?

Use this time between dates calculator to find the exact duration between two dates in total days, business days, weeks, months, and hours. Pick a preset or enter dates, choose date-only or date-time mode, then calculate to get a copy-ready summary plus a detailed breakdown.

Keep the decision-making view simple: the page puts the headline duration and business days first, then leaves the longer breakdowns and planning notes below.

How to Guide

  1. Choose Dates only for day-based planning or Date & time for exact hour and minute gaps.
  2. Use a quick preset or enter the start and end points manually.
  3. Turn on Include end date when the final calendar day should count in planning totals.
  4. Click Calculate difference to refresh the headline answer, breakdown tiles, and detail lists.

When to use dates only, date & time, or business days

Dates only is the better choice when the calendar day itself is the important part of the answer. It works well for leave windows, project timelines, delivery planning, and travel spans where the question is about whole days, weeks, or months rather than exact hours and minutes. Date & time mode is better when the precise elapsed interval matters, such as appointments, filings, launches, or countdown-style deadlines.

Business days are useful when weekends should not count toward the working window. That makes the calculator more practical for service-level expectations, internal deadlines, and planning tasks where Saturday and Sunday do not move the process forward. The point is not to choose one answer format forever, but to choose the one that matches the decision you are making.

  • Dates only: best for calendar planning and whole-day comparisons.
  • Date & time: best for exact elapsed intervals where hours and minutes matter.
  • Business days: best when weekends should not count toward the usable window.

FAQ

How do I calculate the days between two dates?

Enter the start and end dates, then calculate. The page gives the headline answer first and the supporting totals underneath.

Does this page show business days?

Yes. Business days exclude Saturdays and Sundays, which is useful for work and delivery planning.

What does Include end date change?

It adds the final calendar day to day-based planning counts, which is often what people want for leave, trips, and schedules.

Why can months and total days tell different stories?

Months follow the real calendar, while days and hours measure elapsed time directly. Both are shown because both are useful.

What if the start date is after the end date?

The calculator does not block you. It labels the range as reversed and shows the absolute duration.

Does daylight saving time affect the result?

Yes, in Date & time mode the answer follows your device clock, so clock changes can affect total hours and minutes.

Why does the page show business days near the top?

Many planning decisions depend on working days first, so business days stay close to the headline answer.

Can I copy a summary for email or notes?

Yes. Use Copy summary to capture the main range and the most useful supporting numbers in one clean line.

When should I use a preset?

Presets are useful when you want a fast example or a common planning range before you fine-tune the dates yourself.

Are my dates stored?

No. All calculations run locally in your browser - no data is stored.
