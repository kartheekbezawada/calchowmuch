FAQ

Questions people ask before trusting the answer

How do I calculate the days between two dates?

Enter the start and end dates, then calculate. The page gives the headline answer first and the supporting totals underneath.

Does this page show business days?

Yes. Business days exclude Saturdays and Sundays, which is useful for work and delivery planning.

What does Include end date change?

It adds the final calendar day to day-based planning counts, which is often what people want for leave, trips, and schedules.

Why can months and total days tell different stories?

Months follow the real calendar, while days and hours measure elapsed time directly. Both are shown because both are useful.

What if the start date is after the end date?

The calculator does not block you. It labels the range as reversed and shows the absolute duration.

Does daylight saving time affect the result?

Yes, in Date & time mode the answer follows your device clock, so clock changes can affect total hours and minutes.
