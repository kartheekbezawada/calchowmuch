Frequently Asked Questions

01

How does a PCP calculator estimate monthly payment?

It uses financed amount, APR, term, GFV, and option fee to estimate equal monthly installments.

02

What is GFV in a PCP agreement?

GFV is the deferred value due at the end if you choose to purchase the vehicle.

03

How does option fee affect final payment?

Final payment equals GFV plus option fee, so a higher fee raises the amount due at term end.

04

Can deposit be entered as amount or percent?

Yes. Use the deposit toggle to switch between absolute amount and percentage input.

05

Can term be switched between years and months?

Yes. The term unit toggle converts values between years and months without recalculating until Calculate.

06

Does editing sliders recalculate immediately?

No. Results and explanation values update only after clicking Calculate PCP.

07

Why are PCP monthly payments usually lower?

Part of the principal is deferred to the final payment, so less principal is repaid during monthly installments.

08

What does total payable include in PCP?

Total payable includes deposit, all monthly payments, and the final payment including option fee.

09

Can GFV and option fee exceed financed amount?

No. Final payment is constrained so it cannot exceed the financed balance.

10

Are these PCP results lender-specific quotations?

No. These are fixed-rate estimates and can differ from lender offers and contract terms.
