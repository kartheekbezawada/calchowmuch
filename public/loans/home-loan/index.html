Mortgage Payment Overview

A mortgage payment repays the home loan balance plus interest over time. Early payments are interest-heavy because interest is calculated on the larger starting balance. As the balance falls, more of each payment goes toward principal.

Extra payments reduce the outstanding balance faster, which shortens the payoff timeline and lowers total interest paid.
