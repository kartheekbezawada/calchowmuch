Frequently Asked Questions

01

How does a hire purchase calculator estimate monthly payment?

It uses financed amount, APR, term, and final balloon to estimate equal monthly installments.

02

What is amount financed in hire purchase?

Amount financed is vehicle price minus deposit before interest is applied.

03

How does a balloon payment affect monthly installments?

A larger balloon usually lowers monthly installments but leaves more principal due at the end.

04

Can balloon payment be higher than financed amount?

No. Balloon is capped at the financed amount in this calculator.

05

Why does a longer term reduce monthly payment?

Longer terms spread repayment across more months, but can increase total interest paid.

06

Can I switch between years and months for term input?

Yes. Use the term unit toggle to enter duration in years or months.

07

Does changing input values recalculate automatically?

No. Results update when you click the Calculate Hire Purchase button.

08

What does total payable include?

Total payable includes deposit, all monthly installments, and the final balloon payment.

09

Is this suitable for comparing different deposit scenarios?

Yes. Adjust deposit and recalculate to compare installment and total payable tradeoffs.

10

Are these lender-specific repayments?

No. These are fixed-rate estimates and may differ from lender disclosures and fees.
