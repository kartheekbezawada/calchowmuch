Privacy Policy

Effective date: 27 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CalcHowMuch ("we", "us") handles information when you use our calculators and informational pages (the "Site"). We are an independent project site and do not intend to collect personal information from you for calculator use. We use a small number of third-party services to deliver the Site and fund it through advertising.

1) What we do and do not do

We do not:

  • Require accounts or logins to use calculators.
  • Ask you to provide personal details (name, email, phone, address) to use calculators.
  • Sell your personal data.

We do:

  • Use infrastructure services (Cloudflare) to host, secure, and deliver the Site, which involves processing basic technical request data, such as IP address and device/browser information.
  • Show ads via Google AdSense to support the Site.

Accordingly, where legally required, we will show a consent notice and provide choices related to advertising cookies and personalisation.

We may use Cloudflare Web Analytics or Cloudflare-level reporting to understand aggregated usage (e.g., page views, high-level device/browser data) and improve performance/content.
