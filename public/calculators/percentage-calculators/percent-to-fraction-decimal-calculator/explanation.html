How Do You Convert a Percent into Decimal and Fraction Form?

Use this calculator to rewrite a percentage in decimal form and as a simplified fraction. It keeps the same value and only changes the representation.

The decimal conversion divides by 100 because percent means per hundred. The fraction conversion writes the same value over 100 and then simplifies it.

How to Guide

A percentage is a ratio out of 100. Converting percent to decimal and fraction is a direct transformation of the same value into different numeric representations.

  • Read the input percent: Current value is 12.5%.
  • Convert to decimal: Divide by 100 to get 0.125.
  • Convert to fraction: Write X over 100, simplify, and get 1/8.
  • Check mixed form: Mixed number is N/A (proper fraction).
  • Track the process: Steps: 12.5% -> 12.5/100 -> 1/8.

Worked Example

If the input is 12.5%, divide by 100 to get the decimal 0.125. For the fraction form, write 12.5 over 100, convert it to an equivalent whole-number fraction, and simplify to 1/8.

How to Interpret Your Result

The decimal, fraction, and mixed number all represent the same value. Choose the decimal form for calculations, the fraction form for exact math, and the mixed number only when the simplified fraction is improper.

How the Formula Works

Decimal = X / 100

Fraction = X / 100 (simplified)

  1. Decimal path: Move the decimal point two places left.
  2. Fraction path: Represent input as X/100.
  3. Simplify: Divide numerator and denominator by the greatest common divisor.
  4. Normalize sign: Negative inputs keep a negative decimal and fraction.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert a percent to a decimal?

Divide the percent by 100.

How do you convert a percent to a fraction?

Write it over 100 and simplify the fraction.

What is 50% as a decimal?

50% equals 0.5.

What is 25% as a fraction?

25% equals 1/4.

How do you simplify the percent fraction?

Divide the numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor.

How do you convert a decimal percent like 12.5% to a fraction?

Convert 12.5 to 125/10, then divide by 100 to get 125/1000 and simplify.

Can a percent be written as a mixed number fraction?

Yes, if the fraction is improper, it can be written as a mixed number.

Do negative percentages work the same way?

Yes, the negative sign carries through to the decimal and fraction.

Why does converting percent to decimal use 100?

Percent means per hundred, so dividing by 100 converts it to a per-one value.

Where is percent to fraction conversion used?

It is used in math education, rates, discounts, and quick equivalence checks.
