Why This Countdown Is Simple

This countdown is built for one job: show time left clearly. You enter a name, pick a target date and time, and start the timer.

The first screen stays focused so users do not need to scan multiple cards or settings before they can begin.

How to Use It

1) Enter an event name. 2) Pick a future date and time. 3) Press Start countdown.

If you want a faster setup, open Presets & options and choose a yearly date like New Year, Christmas, or Thanksgiving. You can also pick an event type and theme to customize the preview.

What You See After Start

The timer updates live in days, hours, minutes, and seconds using your local device time. Milestones mark key checkpoints such as 30 days left, 7 days left, and the final hour.

When the target time arrives, the timer reaches zero and shows that your event is live. You can copy a plain-language summary or the event date for sharing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this for birthdays, launches, or trips?

Yes. It works for any event where you want a clear live countdown.

Does this use my local time zone?

Yes. The countdown follows your device clock and local time zone.

Can I jump to common yearly dates quickly?

Yes. Open Presets & options and use Quick preset to fill the next upcoming occurrence for common annual dates and holidays.

What happens when the event time arrives?

The countdown reaches zero and shows that the event is live.
