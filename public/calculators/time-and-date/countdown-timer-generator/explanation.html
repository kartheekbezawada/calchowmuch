Live countdowns

How long until an event starts?

Use this timer to count down to launches, birthdays, trips, exams, deadlines, or holiday events with live second-by-second updates, milestone markers, and calendar-share actions.

How to Guide

  1. Choose a region preset for a quick start or type your own event name.
  2. Set the target date and time for the moment you want to track.
  3. Click Start countdown to begin the live timer and milestone tracking.
  4. Use the share and calendar actions after the timer starts to copy or export the event.

Region presets Fast holiday or known-event starters Auto-fills sample events and target dates
Custom event name Personal deadlines and one-off events Lets you label the live timer and share output

FAQ

How do I make a countdown timer for a future date?

Enter the event name, choose the future date and time, then start the timer to see the live countdown.

Does the countdown use my local time zone?

Yes. The timer follows your device clock and local time zone.

Can I add the event to Google Calendar or Outlook?

Yes. After starting the timer, you can send the event to Google Calendar, Outlook, or download an .ics file.

What happens when the timer reaches zero?

The countdown reaches zero and the page marks the event as live.

Important Notes

  • Last updated: March 2026
  • Accuracy: The timer depends on your device clock and local time zone, so results are only as accurate as your device settings.
  • Disclaimer: Use official schedules or calendar confirmations for flights, exams, launches, and other events where exact timing matters.
  • Assumptions: Region-based presets are convenience examples and may change from year to year, so review the auto-filled date and time before relying on them.
  • Privacy: All calculations run locally in your browser - no data is stored.
