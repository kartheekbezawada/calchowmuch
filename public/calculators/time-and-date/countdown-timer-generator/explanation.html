Why Use This Countdown Timer?

  • Shows live time left in days, hours, minutes, and seconds for any future event.
  • Works for birthdays, launches, trips, deadlines, exams, holidays, and milestone dates.
  • Keeps the setup fast while still offering calendar exports and share-ready summaries.

How To Use This Countdown Timer

  • Choose a region and preset event for a quick start, or type a custom event name.
  • Set the target date and time for the moment you want to count down to.
  • Press Start countdown to begin the live timer and milestone tracking.
  • Use the share and calendar actions to copy the summary or export the event after the timer starts.

What The Countdown Shows

  • A live timer that updates every second using your device clock and local time zone.
  • Milestone markers for 30 days, 7 days, 24 hours, and the final hour before the event.
  • A simple event summary plus options for Google Calendar, Outlook, and `.ics` download.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I make a countdown timer for a future date? Enter the event name, choose the future date and time, then start the timer to see the live countdown.
  • Does the countdown use my local time zone? Yes. The timer follows your device clock and local time zone.
  • Can I add the event to Google Calendar or Outlook? Yes. After starting the timer, you can send the event to Google Calendar, Outlook, or download an `.ics` file.
  • What happens when the timer reaches zero? The countdown reaches zero and the page marks the event as live.
