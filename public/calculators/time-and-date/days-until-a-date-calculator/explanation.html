Date planning

How many days until a date?

Use this planner to count down to a future date, measure how long ago a past date happened, or compare two dates with weekday-only and inclusive-count options for schedules, launches, trips, and work planning.

How to Guide

  1. Choose From today for a quick countdown or Date range to compare two custom dates.
  2. Enter the target date, or use a preset to jump to common planning windows.
  3. Turn on Include both dates or Weekdays only when the count needs schedule-specific rules.
  4. Click Calculate to refresh the main result, breakdown cards, planning markers, and share tools.

Common countdown and date-range scenarios

From today mode is the fastest option when the real question is simply how long until something happens or how long ago it happened. It is a practical fit for birthdays, exams, launches, holiday trips, notice periods, and any milestone that naturally uses today as the anchor point. Date range mode is better when both dates are chosen on purpose, such as a project window, itinerary, lease period, or leave request.

Weekdays only matters when calendar days and working days are not interchangeable. That makes it more useful for internal deadlines, staffing plans, or school and office schedules. Include both dates is another small switch with a big effect, because some planning questions count the starting day and some do not. Keeping those options visible makes the final answer easier to trust and easier to explain to someone else.

  • From today: quickest choice for countdowns or days-since questions.
  • Date range: better when both dates are deliberate planning anchors.
  • Weekdays only: better when weekends should not count toward the working window.

FAQ

Can I use this for past dates?

Yes. The result automatically switches to days since the selected date.

What does weekdays only mean?

It excludes Saturdays and Sundays and gives a workweek-style count.

What does include both dates do?

It adds the start date into the count, which is useful for itinerary or schedule planning.

Can I share the result?

Yes. You can copy the summary, copy the dates, export to calendar, or create a share card image.
