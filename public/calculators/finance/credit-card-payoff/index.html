Credit Card Payoff Calculator

Estimate how long it takes to clear a balance with a fixed monthly payment.

Explanation

This estimate assumes a fixed monthly payment applied to your balance at the current APR. Each month, interest is added first, and then your payment reduces the remaining balance.

  • Monthly rate: APR divided by 12.
  • Payoff time: How many months it takes for the balance to reach zero.
  • Total interest: The amount paid above the original balance.

Increase the monthly payment to shorten the payoff timeline and reduce interest.
