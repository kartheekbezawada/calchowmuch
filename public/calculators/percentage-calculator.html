Explanation

Percentages compare a part to a whole. Use the mode tabs to switch between increase, decrease, and percent-of calculations without reloading the page.

  • Increase: (Ending − Starting) ÷ Starting × 100.
  • Decrease: (Starting − Ending) ÷ Starting × 100.
  • What % of what: Part ÷ Whole × 100.
  • What is X% of Y: X ÷ 100 × Y.

Example: Moving from 200 to 260 is a 30% increase because (60 ÷ 200) × 100 = 30%.
