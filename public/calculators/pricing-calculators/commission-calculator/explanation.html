How Much Commission Will the Sale Generate?

Use this calculator to estimate commission earnings from a flat percentage or a tiered plan.

It is useful for sales compensation, brokerage, affiliate arrangements, and any payout model where earnings depend on sales volume.

How to Guide

  1. Choose flat rate or tiered plan mode.
  2. Enter total sales and the rate inputs for the selected mode.
  3. Click Calculate commission to see total commission and effective rate.
  4. In tiered mode, review the summary and breakdown table for each commission band.

At a Glance

  • Mode: Flat Commission %
  • Total sales: $30,000.00
  • Rate(s): 7.50%
  • Total commission: $2,250.00
  • Effective rate: 7.50%

Worked Example

In flat-rate mode, if total sales are $30,000.00 and the rate is 7.50%, commission equals 30,000 x 0.075, which gives $2,250.00. The effective rate is also 7.50% because one flat rate was applied to the whole sale.

In tiered mode, each sales band is priced at its own rate, then the tier payouts are added together to reach the final commission total.

Formula

Commission = Sales x (Rate / 100)

In tiered mode, the calculator applies each rate to the matching sales band and then adds the tier commissions to get the total payout.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a commission calculator?

It estimates payout from sales using a flat rate or tiered commission structure.

How do you calculate flat commission?

Multiply total sales by the commission percentage written as a decimal.

How do tiered commissions work?

Different portions of sales earn different rates based on the tier thresholds.

What is effective commission rate?

It is total commission divided by total sales, expressed as a percentage.

Can commission rates use decimals like 7.5%?

Yes. Decimal commission rates are handled the same way as whole numbers.

What happens when sales are zero?

Total commission becomes zero, and the effective rate may be shown as zero.
