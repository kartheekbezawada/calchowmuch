Logarithmic Scale Converter

Switch between linear measurements and logarithmic scales that quantify ratios (decibels), acidity ( pH), or seismic intensity (Richter magnitude).

  • dB takes 20 · log₁₀(amplitude/reference) for sound/voltage-related ratios. Adjust the reference to compare different baselines.
  • pH simply applies −log₁₀ to hydrogen ion concentration.
  • Richter magnitudes use log₁₀ of ground-motion ratios; you can provide a custom reference amplitude.
