How it works

What this raise calculator shows

This route compares current salary, raise amount, and new salary in one answer-first view. Use percent mode when you know the raise percentage, or amount mode when you know the dollar increase.

  • Percent mode raise amount: current salary × raise percent
  • Amount mode raise amount: the flat amount you enter
  • New salary: current salary + raise amount
  • Percent increase: raise amount ÷ current salary × 100

Worked example

With a $60,000.00 current salary and a 5% raise, the raise amount is $3,000.00 and the new salary is $63,000.00.

Frequently asked questions

How do you calculate a raise?

Add a flat raise amount to current salary, or multiply current salary by the raise percentage.

Can I use a percent raise or a flat raise amount?

Yes. This route supports both modes.

Does this show my new salary after the raise?

Yes. The main result is the new salary after applying the raise.

Can I see the raise amount separately?

Yes. The route also shows the raise amount and the resulting percent increase.

Does this include taxes?

No. This route estimates gross pay changes only.
